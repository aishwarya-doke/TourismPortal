Sandhan Valley Rappelling & Trek

Trek Details
Location: Ahmednagar
Max Altitude: 4,100 ft
Trek Difficulty: Moderate
Duration: 1N/2D
Base Village: Samrad
Best Season: Anytime except summer

Trek Overview

Embark on this fun trek to the Valley of Shadows situated at an altitude of 4100 ft. Enjoy the mesmerizing view of sunset and sunrise from the Konkankada View Point.
Experience thrill while trying 45 feet rappelling at multiple points in the 200 ft deep water-carved valley. Witness the outstanding views of peaks such as Alang, Madangad, Kulang, Ratangad, Ajoba & Kalsubai.


Know More

- The start point of this trek is Samrad Village and the endpoint is Dehne Village.
- An experienced leader will guide you throughout the trek and will help you with the basics and safety measures required especially for Rappelling.
- This trip will have all the best experiences you can have. There are around 100 ft of rocky patches where you have to do Sandhan valley rappelling with a complete guide and equipment.
- The minimum age requirement for the package is 15 years of age. There will be a professional guide to assist you throughout the whole trek.
- Curb your hunger with morning breakfast served at the base village. Grab a delicious lunch to recharge your energy in the noon.
