Kalsubai Night Trek

Trek Details
Location: Igatpuri
Max Altitude: 5,400 ft
Trek Difficulty: Moderate
Duration: 1N, 2D
Base Village: Bari
Best Season: Monsoon and Winter

Trek Overview

Embark on the highest peak of Maharashtra- Kalsubai Peak standing at a height of 5400 Ft. Witness views of peaks such as Alang, Madangad, Kulang, Ratangad, Pabhargad, Ghanchakkar & Harishchandragad from the highest point. Explore rugged trails & waterfalls during this adventurous 6.6 km long trek.
Visit the famous Kalsubai temple during sunrise & observe its magnificent beauty. There are steel railings, chains, and ladders at places where it is difficult to climb.


Know More

- The Kalsubai trek offers a once-in-a-lifetime experience to conquer the highest peak of Maharashtra which is famous for its natural habitat and spellbinding views.
- Grab delicious meals in the form of breakfast and lunch (veg) & taste the local Maharashtrian cuisine.
- Kalsubai Height being the highest peak commands a beautiful view.
- Visit the Kalsubai temple during sunrise which is surrounded by surreal greenery and a blissful environment.
- Curb your hunger with morning breakfast served at the base village. Grab a delicious lunch to recharge your energy in the noon.
- Witness the beauty of the Kalsubai temple when you embark on this trekking expedition.
