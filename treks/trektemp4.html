Harishchandragad-Konkankada

Trek Details
Location: Ahmednagar
Max Altitude: 4,670 ft
Trek Difficulty: Moderate
Duration: 1N, 2D
Base Village: Pachnai
Best Season: Monsoon and Winter

Trek Overview

Embark on this fun trek to the Harishchandragad fort situated at an altitude of 4670 ft. Visit the Konkankada cliff, the 'Saptatirtha' Lake & Harishchandreshwar Temple at the top.
Witness & capture the sights of the mesmerizing sunrise from the Taramati sunrise point. Enjoy a warm bonfire session at the night while camping on the top of Konkankada.


Know More

- Harischandragad, situated in the Malshej region of the Western Ghats is one of the most famous hill forts in the region.
- Also, witness the Temple of Harishchandreshwar and Konkan Kada which provides a scenic view of the surrounding region.
- The experience of scenic view beauty from the top is excellent and extraordinary.
- Harishchandragad trek has multiple routes. It has easy routes for beginner-level trekking, moderate routes for an exciting trekking experience, and difficult routes for a challenging trekking experience. Every trek route is surprising, exciting, and thrilling.
- Curb your hunger with morning breakfast served at the base village. Grab a delicious lunch to recharge your energy in the noon.
- You will get an assistance of a guide during the activity to ease the obstacles so you enjoy every part of the Harishchandragad trek.
