Salher - Salota Trek

Trek Details
Location: Nashik
Max Altitude: 5,141 ft
Trek Difficulty: Moderate
Duration: 1N, 2D
Base Village: Salher Wadi
Best Season: Monsoon and Winter

Trek Overview

Salher is the highest fort in Maharashtra which offers spectacular views of the Baglan mountain range in Nashik. According to a mythical legend, Lord Parshuram did his Tapascharya at Salher Fort. This fort is historically significant due to the Battle of Salher that happened here.
You can plan for a two days trek in which you can stay on Salher fort for a night and then go to Salota [twin fort] the next morning.


Know More

- The Kalsubai trek offers a once-in-a-lifetime experience to conquer the highest peak of Maharashtra which is famous for its natural habitat and spellbinding views.
- Rock Cut Stairs at all forts, 108 Feet Idol of Rishabhdev at Mangi Tungi, Temple at Top of Highest Fort in Maharashtra
- Age group 15 to 50 years
- Visit the Kalsubai temple during sunrise which is surrounded by surreal greenery and a blissful environment.
- Curb your hunger with morning breakfast served at the base village. Grab a delicious lunch to recharge your energy in the noon.
- Parshuram Temple, BEAUTIFUL VIEWS OF THE MOUNTAIN RANGES, STAIRS AND DOORS WHICH ARE STILL INTACT
