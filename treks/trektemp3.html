Irshalgad

Trek Details
Location: Karjat
Max Altitude: 3,700 ft
Trek Difficulty: Moderate
Duration: 1N
Base Village: Irshalwadi
Best Season: Monsoon and Winter

Trek Overview

Irshalgad trek is perfect for beginners. The trek is steep but is manageable even for beginners. rshalgad pinnacle is accessible by an easy to moderate 2 hour trek.
The Irshalgad Fort has been visited by very few history lovers and is a favourite for trekkers, adventurers, and nature photographers as well. This 1-day Irshalgad trekking tour will offer you an ideal escapade, away from the busy city life.
