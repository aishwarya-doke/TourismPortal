Harihar Fort Trek

Trek Details
Location: Nashik
Max Altitude: 3676ft
Trek Difficulty: Medium
Duration: 1N/2D
Base Village: Nirgudpada
Best Season: October to February, June to August

Trek Overview

Embark on this exciting trek to the famous Harihar fort situated at an altitude of 3676 ft. Enjoy a thrilling ascend with the help of iconic steep steps that are almost 80Â° vertically inclined.
Witness views of the peaks such as Bhaskargad, Anjaneri fort, Brahmagiri & Utwad fort. Admire the ancient idols of Lord Hanuman, Shiva, and Nandi on this thrilling trek.


Know More

- The Harihar trek timings are from 12:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
- You can avail of the Harihar trek package with transportation from Mumbai/Pune to Mumbai/Pune by bus. Those opting for without transportation variant will have to reach the base village, Harshewadi or Nirugpada village by 3 AM.
- Enjoy trekking through dense wilderness and enjoy glimpses of surrounding hills in Igatpuri when you embark on the Harihar Fort trek.
The duration of this Harihar trek is one day and one night.
- The Harihar fort trek difficulty level is medium and can be conducted by both beginners and intermediate trekkers. There will be a professional guide to assist you throughout the whole trek.
- This activity is suitable for all age groups, friends, family, and corporate outings.
- Curb your hunger with morning breakfast served at the base village. Grab a delicious lunch to recharge your energy in the noon.
