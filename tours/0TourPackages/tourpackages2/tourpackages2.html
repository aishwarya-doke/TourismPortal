Mahabaleshwar

Tour Overview

Mahabaleshwar is a hill station located in the Western Ghats range. This picturesque destination once served as a summer capital of Mumbai district during the British Raj era. With an average elevation of 1,353 metres Mahabaleshwar is an ideal location to rejuvenate your tiring soul. Mahabaleshwar Panchgani hill station tour takes you to one of the highest hill stations in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra offering spectacular sights to capture in your mind and photograph through your camera lenses.

Elephanta Caves to stunning view of the valleys from Wilson point. See the glittering waters of the Lake Dhom Dam from Sydney Point and also The Lingmala Falls. This breathtaking panoramic outline scatters around the hill station
